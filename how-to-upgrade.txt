To upgrade mesoBoard from previous versions to version 0.9.2 (current version):

1- Backup mesoBoard files and database
2- Open "Settings.config" and modify your database connection information
3- Copy and overwrite files from this folder to your mesoBoard installation folder
4- Go to http://yourdomain.com/Admin/Upgrade and login as an admin
5- Follow the on screen instructions

For questions, comments and support, visit http://mesoBoard.com/Board
